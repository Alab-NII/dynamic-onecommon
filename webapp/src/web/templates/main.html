Welcome!

In this game, you and your partner will be shown different parts of a grid with multiple dots.
At each turn, the dots (and your view) will move randomly on the grid.
Your goal is to chat with your partner to find a common dot at the end of each turn.

Take Tutorial

If you are new to this HIT, please take this tutorial first (4 pages with animation).
It is critical that you read, understand and follow all the instructions very carefully!

Start Chat!

If you've understood how the game works, let's start playing!
You will be rewarded based on the number of successful turns.

  • 2 turns: $0.50
  • 3 turns: $0.75 + $0.05
  • 4 turns: $1.00 + $0.10
  • 5 turns: $1.25 + $0.25

Please be aware that we review all dialogues carefully and judge acceptance based on dialogue quality.
Your bonus may not be provided or your HIT may not be accepted if you do not follow all instructions carefully.



Some browser may not play our animations properly (e.g. Firefox).
If you face any trouble, please try using a different browser (e.g. Google Chrome, Safari).
